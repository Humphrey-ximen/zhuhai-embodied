Positioning

Zhuhai Embodied Systems is an independent engineering studio focused on embodied control, embedded systems, and simulation-driven development. Work is documented as an evolving archive: clear artifacts, clear status, and clear trajectory.

Principles

• Minimal, auditable artifacts
• Iteration over hype
• Verification as a habit
• Systems thinking across hardware and software

Open Source Policy

Unless restricted by confidentiality obligations (e.g., NDAs) or third-party rights, work produced under Zhuhai Embodied Systems is open-sourced by default. When details cannot be disclosed, the archive will provide high-level descriptions focused on engineering principles, verification methods, and learnings—without exposing confidential implementation.

Confidentiality

Some industry experience is summarized at a high level to respect confidentiality agreements. No proprietary source code, internal metrics, customer identifiers, or non-public product details are included.
